About us

Discover A Lakeside Escape

Discover A Lakeside Escape

Luxury independent five-star hotel founded in 1875, member of Preferred Hotels & Resorts, Le Richemond is a legend in Geneva. Ideally set on the shores of Lake Geneva, Le Richemond offers a breathtaking view on the emblematic Jet d’Eau and the highest Europe mountain Mont Blanc. In 2019, Le Richemond achieved a Five-Star Award by Forbes Travel Guide.

Le Jardin

Le Jardin

At Le Jardin, Chef Philippe Bourrel shares a stylish, light and fresh French cuisine featuring the best of local products. A unique experience starting from the amuse-bouche to Sébastien Quazzola's dessert creations, Pastry Chef of the Year 2019.

Le Jardin

NEW ! Le Jardin is opened everyday, from Monday to Sunday for lunch (12pm-2pm) and dinner (7pm-10pm).

Recognized as “Ambassador of The Geneva Region” and listed in the prestigious Gault & Millau guide with the grade of 17/20, Le Jardin is an unmissable address in Geneva. Our sommelier has created an excellent wine list that accents the richness and quality of vineyards near Geneva and around Switzerland.

Everyday for lunch, discover our market menu and our winter menu.

Veggie mania

Vegetables are the stars of this 100% creative and delicious veggie menu. Enjoy the leeks from the garden in salt crust as well as the thin tart of porcini mushroom, the crapaudine beet ravioli and the candied apple.
